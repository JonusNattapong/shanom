<business_impact_framework>
Translate technical severity to business impact:

**Critical (CVSS 9.0-10.0)**
- Business Translation: "Data Breach Imminent"
- Financial Impact: $500K - $5M+ (breach notification, fines, lawsuits)
- Operational Impact: System compromise, data exfiltration, service disruption
- Reputational Impact: Severe - media coverage, customer loss, partner confidence
- Regulatory: GDPR/CCPA violations, sector-specific penalties

**High (CVSS 7.0-8.9)**
- Business Translation: "Significant Security Exposure"
- Financial Impact: $100K - $500K (incident response, remediation)
- Operational Impact: Unauthorized access, data manipulation
- Reputational Impact: High - customer notification may be required
- Regulatory: Potential reporting obligations

**Medium (CVSS 4.0-6.9)**
- Business Translation: "Security Gap Requiring Attention"
- Financial Impact: $10K - $100K (remediation, monitoring)
- Operational Impact: Limited unauthorized actions, information disclosure
- Reputational Impact: Moderate - manageable with proper response
- Regulatory: Generally no immediate reporting required

**Low (CVSS 0.1-3.9)**
- Business Translation: "Defense in Depth Opportunity"
- Financial Impact: <$10K (preventive improvements)
- Operational Impact: Minimal direct risk
- Reputational Impact: Low
- Regulatory: Best practice compliance
</business_impact_framework>

<compliance_mapping>
Map findings to common compliance frameworks:

**PCI DSS (if payment data involved)**
- Critical: Req 6.5.1 (Injection), 6.5.10 (Broken Auth)
- High: Req 8.2 (Strong Auth), 6.5.7 (XSS)

**GDPR (if EU data subjects)**
- Critical: Article 32 (Security of processing)
- High: Article 25 (Data protection by design)
- Breach notification: Article 33-34 (72-hour rule)

**SOC 2 Type II**
- Critical: CC6.1 (Logical access), CC7.2 (System monitoring)
- High: CC6.6 (Encryption), CC6.7 (Transmission security)

**ISO 27001**
- Critical: A.9.4 (Access control), A.12.3 (Backup)
- High: A.10.1 (Cryptography), A.12.4 (Logging)

**NIST CSF**
- Critical: PR.AC (Access control), PR.DS (Data security)
- High: DE.AE (Anomalies), RS.MI (Mitigation)
</compliance_mapping>
